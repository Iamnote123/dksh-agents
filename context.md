## Retail Channels

| Channel | Description | Primary accounts |
|---|---|---|
| **Hypermarket** | Large-format modern trade | Lotus's, Big C |
| **Supermarket** | Mid-format modern trade | Tops (primary); Gourmet (secondary) |

| **GT** | General / traditional trade | Wholesalers, sub-distributors |
| **E-commerce** | Online platforms | Lazada, Shopee |
| **Van** | Direct van sales | Analyzed **separately** — own KPI set |

> **Van vs Non-Van:** always analyzed separately. Van runs on NIS/salesman, route productivity, and loading — not the standard Non-Van chain.

---

## Key KPIs

| KPI | Definition | Threshold / note |
|---|---|---|
| **NIS** | Net Invoice Sales — sell-in revenue to trade | Exclude one-time shipments from recurring/trend NIS |
| **IMS** | Inventory Movement Sales — sell-out from trade | — |
| **DOH** | Days on Hand — inventory coverage in days | > 120 days = urgent |
| **CFR** | Case Fill Rate — order fulfilment performance | — |
| **TI** | Trade Investment — promo & channel spend | Tracked as % of NIS |
| **MAPE** | Mean Absolute Percentage Error — forecast accuracy | ≤ 15% good · > 30% high risk |
| **GP** | Gross Profit — margin after COGS | Finance Analyst owns |
| **ROI** | Return on trade/promo investment | ≥ 1.5 approve · < 1.0 reject (unless strategic) |

## Communication Standards

### Always
- Focus on business impact and financial consequence
- State assumptions explicitly; use realistic assumptions grounded in data
- Identify risk clearly with financial consequence
- Identify root cause — not just symptoms
- Prioritize executable strategy
- Retailer-specific output — never generic across accounts

### Never
- Generic consulting wording ("leverage synergies," "holistic approach")
- Ignore inventory issues or retailer limitations
- Use overly optimistic forecasts
- Invent numbers silently
- Deliver output with known QA issues
